About

👋 关于 Echoes & Edges

欢迎来到 Echoes & Edges，在这里，我将生活中的点滴、职业旅程、灵感火花和编码的乐趣汇聚于一处。

通过文字，我梳理思绪，提炼经验，分享个人成长的轨迹。

感谢你的驻足——愿这些文字能带给你灵感、共鸣与点燃自己旅程的火花。

💡 关于我

我是 一名 Java 开发者，常驻 陕西西安——一座兼具历史底蕴与创新活力的城市。
每天我编写 Java 代码、解决问题，并打造各种小工具，以让生活更便捷。
而在屏幕之外，我始终保持好奇，持续学习。

🎧📚🏃 我的热爱

  • 🎵 音乐 激发创意——无论是古典钢琴、爵士即兴，还是最新的独立乐队。
  • 📖 阅读 让我谦逊并不断获取灵感——阅读范围从技术书籍、编程指南到哲学与小说。
  • 🏃 运动 保持身心平衡——清晨沿西安城墙慢跑，周末徒步，以及偶尔的羽毛球对战。

🤝 联系我

欢迎访问我的 GitHub，或通过邮箱与我联系。
无论是关于代码、想法，还是一次简单的问候，我都很乐意倾听。

👋 About Echoes & Edges

Welcome to Echoes & Edges, a space where life’s little moments, my professional journey, sparks of reflection, and the joy of coding come together.

Here, through words, I organize my thoughts, distill experience, and share the path of my personal growth.

Thank you for stopping by—in these lines, may you find inspiration, a moment of connection, and a spark to light your own journey.

💡 About Me

I’m a Java developer based in Xi’an, Shaanxi—a city steeped in history and innovation.
Every day I write Java code, solve problems, and build small tools to make life a bit easier.
But beyond the screen, I’m always curious, always learning.

🎧📚🏃 My Passions

  • 🎵 Music fuels my creativity—whether I’m diving into classical piano, jazz improvisation, or the latest indie bands.
  • 📖 Reading keeps me humble and inspired—from tech books and programming guides to philosophy and fiction.
  • 🏃 Sports keep me grounded—morning jogs along the Xi’an city wall, weekend hikes, and the occasional badminton match.

🤝 Let’s Connect

Feel free to check out my work on GitHub or drop me a line via email.
Whether it’s code, ideas, or just a hello—I’d love to hear from you.
